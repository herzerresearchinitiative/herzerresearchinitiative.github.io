Our Team

Our Team

Our research area is concerned with the development of mathematical models for any energy system, simulate the system using the models and obtain the optimum parameters from the simulation to design the system for optimal performance.
They include (but not limited to):  energy systems modeling, simulation and design, renewable energy systems, flow and heat transfer, and thermal fluidic-induced instability in energy systems.
