About Us

Our Vision

To be the leading international research initiative that provides value-driven, practical solutions to the societal problems in engineering, environment, health, energy, and information technology, which will enable the society to acquire, manage, and execute educationally relevant and economically sound research programs.

Our Mission

  • To carry out research and intellectual endeavours of the highest international standards that advance knowledge and have immediate or long-range practical significance in the society.
  • To create an outstanding environment conducive for intellectual and research growth through interdisciplinary research training, mentoring and sufficient supports from a community of innovative researchers.
  • To promote and facilitate leading-edge collaborative and interdisciplinary research in science, engineering and technology.
  • To enhance research capacity and provide an effective and efficient research programs in sciences, engineering and technology.
  • To accelerate the advancement of knowledge and increase innovation through the development of new technology applicable to the health, energy and power sectors.
  • To conduct multidisciplinary research and development solutions in environment, health, energy, nanotechnology and information technology to meet societal demands.
  • To develop networks with researchers in various research centers, organizations and institutions in the public and private sectors, locally, nationally and internationally.
  • To act as the nexus between the academic institutions and the community with respect to research initiatives of mutual benefits.
  • To produce seasoned academics and researchers equipped with the highest international research tools and standards that advance knowledge in various fields of learning.
  • To develop mutually beneficial linkages with industry in order to develop partnerships and collaborative research.
  • To transfer knowledge to the society through outreach collaborative research; seminars; workshops; lectures; websites; publications and technology transfer.

Diversity

We pride ourselves on a team of diverse young talents collaborating on research that matters.

Herzer Research Initiative values a wide range of opinions, cultures, communities, perspectives and experiences, all of which challenge a student's own beliefs, intellectual passions, views and understanding of the world. Our diverse setting enables students to investigate and engage in current issues and more profound societal questions. As a result, our members discover how they can positively impact the world around them. We are proud to welcome you to explore our team.
